# Workforce Analytics: Employee Performance, Attrition & Productivity Insights

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee workforce data to identify the major factors influencing employee attrition, performance, and productivity. The project follows a complete end-to-end data analytics workflow using Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI to transform raw HR data into meaningful business insights.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition trends
- Identify departments with high attrition
- Study the impact of salary, overtime, and job satisfaction
- Analyze employee demographics
- Build an interactive HR analytics dashboard
- Generate actionable business recommendations

## Dataset

## Key Business Insights

- Employees working overtime show significantly higher attrition.
- Research & Development has the largest workforce.
- Lower job satisfaction is associated with higher attrition.
- Employees with poor work-life balance are more likely to leave.
- Salary differences influence employee retention.
- Business travel frequency impacts attrition.

---

## Recommendations

- Reduce excessive overtime.
- Improve employee engagement.
- Increase career development opportunities.
- Enhance work-life balance initiatives.
- Review salary structures periodically.
- Conduct regular employee satisfaction surveys.
